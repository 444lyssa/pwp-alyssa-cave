PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

  • Purpose:

    For my personal website project I want to create a website for customers to inquire booking a PhotoBooth to have at their next celebration.

  • Audience:

    My target audience will be party-goers, event planners, or anyone who wants to have a PhotoBooth at their party.

  • Goal:

    Showcase my business and book my PhotoBooth at events.

  • Notes:

    Be careful not to get to into making the website complicated, a simple place that describes what we do and to leave contact information.

Persona

  • Name: Mary Poppins

  • Description:

    Technology: Mary Poppins is proficient at using both Apple and Android technology. She mostly uses her iPhone to search the web unless she's at work on her desktop which uses the latest version of Microsoft.

    Behaviors and attitudes: Mary is looking to throw birthday party for her soon-to-be 18-year-old daughter. She has been on the internet looking for cool things that can entertain her guests at a reasonable price. She is also looking for a way to capture the moment without having to be stuck on her phone, camera or anything else that might pull her away from the fun. Mary wants to make sure that she can have fun at the birthday too, so she is definitely looking for something that takes care of itself and doesn't need to be set up or put away.

    Goal: Book simple, reasonably priced, and low maintenance entertainment for her daughter's 18th birthday.
